V1 Index Comparison Explorer

One frozen corpus, three index conditions. IDX-D is the deterministic Markdown-heading tree (no generated summaries — by design). IDX-C adds summaries from a cloud model; IDX-O adds summaries from a local Ollama model. Structure was verified at build time: . Your job here is judging the summaries: are they faithful, specific, and boundary-respecting?

Workflow

  • Pick a node in the tree, or search (/). The center shows IDX-C and IDX-O summaries; the right pane shows the Markdown source for the node's lines.
  • Flag each summary: Faithful · Too generic · Misleading · Missing distinction · Boundary problem · Needs review. Flags save to this browser's localStorage — export to JSON before clearing browser data.
  • Signals only filters to nodes where deterministic heuristics found something worth a look (numbers or entities absent from source, near-duplicate or generic phrasing, …). Signals are prompts for review, not verdicts.

Keyboard

  • j/k next / previous node · n next unreviewed summary · e expand/collapse
  • c/o choose condition card · 1–6 toggle flags · / search · ? this help

Alignment states

  • exact — node ID, title, and line range agree across all three indexes.
  • probable — matched with minor differences (e.g. by heading path, or line drift).
  • divergent / ambiguous — matched but titles differ, or duplicate IDs made the match uncertain.
  • unmatched — no counterpart found; never silently dropped.
